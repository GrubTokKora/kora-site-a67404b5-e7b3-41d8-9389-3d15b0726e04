Our Story

Opened in December 2024, Maska is the newest culinary gem in Hartsdale, bringing the rich and diverse flavors of Indian street food to Westchester County.

Brought to you by the experienced team behind NH 44 Indian, Jaipore Express, Vega Mexican Cuisine, Tako Mexican Street Food, and Lemak Malaysian, we blend decades of restaurant expertise with authentic Indian traditions.

Specializing in quick, flavorful, and authentic street-style Indian cuisine, we serve up classics like crispy beet cutlets, tangy chaat, and hearty paneer kathi rolls with a modern twist.

  • Authentic Indian street food recipes
  • Fresh, high-quality ingredients
  • Vegetarian & vegan-friendly
  • Late-night dining available
