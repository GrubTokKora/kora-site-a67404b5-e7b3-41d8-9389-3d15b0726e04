A Taste of India's Bustling Streets

Hartsdale, NY, has a new culinary gem for lovers of bold flavors and plant-based eats! Maska, a vibrant Indian restaurant, brings the rich and diverse flavors of Indian street food to Westchester. Specializing in quick, flavorful, and authentic street-style Indian cuisine.

Brought to you by experienced restaurateurs, Maska blends tradition with innovation, ensuring a memorable dining experience with every bite. Whether you're dining in or grabbing a bite on the go, expect bold flavors, fresh ingredients, and a vibrant atmosphere that celebrates India's rich culinary heritage.

"Come experience Maska – where every bite is a taste of India’s bustling streets, now in the heart of Hartsdale!"
